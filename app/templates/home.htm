Innov8 Coworking

Innov8’s design and value had to be incorporated, which helped us create a plan of action for Innov8, swiftly and efficiently!

apartment

15,000 Sq Ft reinvented in 90 Days

Co-Working Spaces are the next big thing in the Commercial Real Estate work and we’re excited to have created a masterpiece in the Co-Working Space in India!

When Mr. Ritesh came to us with his vision of converting the MIDC building into a co-working hub, his conviction in converting this old building into a new-age working space - was infectious! The brief given to us was direct and simple - Innov8’s design and value had to be incorporated, which helped us create a plan of action for Innov8, swiftly and efficiently!

We started off with understanding what Innov8 stands for, the kind of culture and working environment it wishes to provide to its member community and the importance of treating this as a commercial, yet fun working space!

While working on this project, we faced many obstacles owing to the age and state of the MIDC building - seepage of Mumbai rainwater being one concern - but by the end of the execution, we were proud to see the MIDC building being restored to its former glory! It was pure joy for Dreamz Unwired to see a new page being unturned in the History of Interior Designing and Construction in India - and a welcome one!

Heaven in Gurgaon

apartment

80,000 Sq Ft reinvented till date.

Ankur Rajgarhia subscribes to the notion that less is more when it comes to interior design. “Our style is grounded in elegance when we built a home” he explains. “I believe that simple and elegant home design leads to ease of living. No fuss, You-friendly!”

This philosophy underlies all of Dreamz Unwired’s projects, and it’s what attracted a couple building a custom home in Gurgaon. They brought in the Dreamz Team in at the start of the process, asking them to work to create an elegant yet comfortable abode that capitalizes on its bucolic surroundings.

The house is located in a high-rise in the middle of the concrete City, retaining unimpeded views while making the abode into a peaceful haven, was a high priority. The living space opens to a porch which looks out into the lit Gurgaon nightline, and if you’re lucky - a starry night - making it a perfect space for the working couple to unwind with a Glass of Wine!

To delineate the kitchen and sitting area, the Dream Team designed a granite-topped, walnut display shelf, using custom Shaker-style cabinetry, marble counters and backsplash. The kitchen opens to a breakfast nook where a rustic roundtable is artfully paired with bright red chairs, to add a pop of colour in the otherwise pristine and state-of-the-art kitchen, which is stunning and exemplifies the team’s expertise. Dream Team also designed a customised cabinet that beautifully displays the couple’s curated collection along one wall.

Most of the furniture is new to the house. An existing sofa was reupholstered for the sitting area to create a “simple, personal space with a touch of modern,” Ankur adds.

Rent Ready

apartment

1,00,000 Sq Ft reinvented in 110 houses till date.

The Rent Ready line was Akur Rajgarhia’s brain child - with the growing number of high-rises in the country, Dream Team decided to start a line meant to recreate spaces in a fixed budget, using quality products, creating a home for individuals and families living away from their homes.

Ankur and the team was approached by the owner of a flat in a highrise in Bangalore. Their brief was clear - they owned a flat, newly constructed and wanted to rent it out to a family of 4, for which they approached Dream Team to transform the space in a limited budget.

Ankur brainstormed with his team and decided to start with transforming the open kitchen space in the flat, using marble top for the surface, cabinets for the walls and designing spaces for the electronic appliances to be fitted in the kitchen space!

In addition to this, the Team customised a TV cabinet with adjoining shelves for books and created beautiful bedrooms for the families.

The owner of the flat was delighted to see the transformation of the property and connected us to 4 more people in the same high-rise to recreate their spaces. We really do have a Dream team!
